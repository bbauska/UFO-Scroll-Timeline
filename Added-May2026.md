DONE - 1952 August 25, U.S., Pittsburg, KS.
Bill Squire, of radio station KOAM saw a disc-shaped object in a field and saw four persons in it.
A bluish light filled the interior. It was 75 ft. long and 10 ft. thick. He was less than 100 ft. 
away as it ascended vertically. Sound was heard "like a covey of quail." Weeds and grass were 
crushed and blown over a wide area. (Kansas Newsletter)

DONE - 1952 August 27  U.S., Lumberton, NC.
Gabriel Durocher and 4 other people independently reported seeing an unusual object. Durocher was 
walking home when he saw the object in a field. The object was glowing blue-white and 30 ft. away.
Blue, yellow and red sparks were seen as the object started to spin and ascend. It ascended vertically.
It was about 30 ft. in diameter and 10 ft. thick. A depressed area was found at the site. 
(The Robesonian, Lumberton, NC.)
